When you know how to make this sauce, you have a powerfull toll in your hands to shine in family or friends meetings

  • Basil
  • Olive oil
  • Parmisan cheese
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Nuts
  1. Using a blender, throw into it the olive oil, salt, black pepper and the basil leaves.
  2. Pulse the blender until you have a leafy texture, do not overblend it!
  3. Then throw the nuts and blend a little more, but take care to not turn the pesto in something too homogenous.
  4. Last but no least, add the parmisan and mix whit a spoon
