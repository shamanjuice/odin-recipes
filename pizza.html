  • Wheat 00 flour, salt, yeast, water and olive oil for the dough
  • Pomodori pelatti. salt, sugar, olive oil and basil for the tomato sauce
  • Cheese, meat, parmisan cheese, parsley and pepper for the toppings
  1. The first thing to do is to make your pizza dough. If you don't know how to do it, the recipe will come in future recipes here in this site!
  2. Stretch your dough ball with your hands, pressing from the center towards the edges, pressing gently taking care to not touch the edges.
  3. Once you stretched your dough ball, you can add a good amount of tomato sauce ~80 grams.
  4. You can now add the toppings. That's the time to show creativity and style
  5. Bake it in a pre-heated oven with aproximatelly 420 degrees Celsius for about 2 minutes
